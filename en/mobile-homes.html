sell conditions

Each mobile home (24 sqm) can host 2 to 6 people.They are composed by a double bedroom, a twin bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen with extractable dinette (1 or 2 beds), and bathroom with shower/WC.They are not provided with bedlinen, kitchen and bath towels.

The facilities are available from 3 pm. on the day of arrival and must be vacated no later than 10:00 am. on the day of departure.

Mobile home and cooking corner must be left clean; otherwise € 40,00 should be paid for the cleaning.

Please note that full payment of your entire stay will be collected on arrival.

No refunds will be paid in the event of late arrival or early departure.

The camping rules applies also for mobile homes guests.

Pets are not allowed

booking

Mobile homes can be reserved only for 3 or more days.To finalize the booking process we will ask a deposit.

cancellation

If you cancel your reservation at least 14 days before your arrival your deposit will be refunded, except for €10.00 as cancellation fees.If you cancel your reservation within 14 days before your arrival you will not be refunded.

late arrival

If you don not arrive before 11.00 a.m. of the day after the day you are due to arrive,your reservation will be automatically cancelled.
